Country Overview: Tanzania

Tanzania is a beautiful country located in East Africa, known for its vast wilderness areas. These include the plains of Serengeti National Park, a safari mecca inhabited by the "big five" game (elephant, lion, leopard, buffalo, and rhino), and Kilimanjaro National Park, home to Africa's highest mountain.

The country is also rich in culture and history, with cities like Zanzibar offering a glimpse into a rich Swahili heritage. Whether you seek an adventure into the wild or want to relax on pristine beaches, Tanzania has something for everyone.
